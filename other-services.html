FAQs

Common Questions

Answers to what people ask before booking.

What specialized services do you offer?
Beyond standard repairs, we connect you with workshops specializing in EV and hybrid diagnostics, ECU and sensor troubleshooting, professional ceramic coating and PPF, and vehicle fitness testing for compliance purposes.
What are Telematics services and why do I need them?
Telematics combines GPS tracking with real-time vehicle data. It lets you monitor fuel consumption, track live location for theft recovery, and receive engine health alerts on your smartphone — useful for both private owners and fleet operators.
Will installing GPS or telematics void my car's warranty?
Our partners primarily use OBD-compliant, plug-and-play installations that do not require cutting any original wiring. This means your manufacturer warranty stays intact while you gain advanced security features.
What cosmetic services protect against Nepal's environment?
We offer 9H ceramic coating for long-lasting gloss, paint protection film (PPF) to prevent stone chips, and underbody anti-rust coating — critical for preventing chassis corrosion on Nepal's dusty and monsoon-heavy roads.
How do I book a service?
Fill out the service request form below with your vehicle details and preferred service. You can opt for pickup and drop-off from your home or office. We confirm next steps within one business day.
Are your service providers verified?
We only partner with VAT and PAN registered workshops and service centers. Where applicable, they are authorized by the NADA Automobiles Association of Nepal or the DOTM. User ratings are also available to help you choose.
Are there package or bundled deals available?
Yes. Many providers offer combo packages — for example, interior deep cleaning bundled with exterior waxing, or GPS tracking with fuel sensors — typically at 10–15% savings compared to booking each separately.
How quickly does roadside assistance arrive?
Within Kathmandu Valley, typical response time is 30–60 minutes. For locations outside the valley, our dispatch team will confirm an estimated arrival before you confirm the request.

These offers apply when you buy a car or book services through AutoViindu. No hidden conditions — just straightforward value for our customers.

We collect your vehicle from your home or office for servicing and return it when done — no need to rearrange your day.
Free Ceramic Coating or Underbody Coating
Get a free ceramic coat or underbody anti-rust treatment on select service packages — protecting your car's finish from day one.
Dashcam Installation
Complimentary dashcam fitting through our partner network when bundled with select vehicle service or purchase packages.
Paint Protection Film (PPF)
PPF installation offered as part of select cosmetic bundles — keeps your paint safe from stone chips and scratches on Nepal's roads.
Loan Processing Fee Waiver
When you arrange vehicle finance through AutoViindu, our banking partners waive the processing fee — saving you money upfront.
Festival Bonus & Cash Discounts
Special discounts and festival bonuses on vehicle purchases and services during major Nepali festivals. Ask us what is currently available.
Fuel Vouchers
Fuel vouchers are included with select new car purchases or major service bookings — so you drive away without stopping at the pump.
Free Accessories Upgrade
Choose from a selection of accessories — floor mats, seat covers, phone mounts, and more — bundled free with qualifying purchases.
Referral Bonus
Refer a friend who buys or books a service through AutoViindu and earn a referral reward. The more you refer, the more you earn.
Offers vary by provider and package
Not all extras apply to every transaction. When you submit a service request or car inquiry, our team will confirm which offers are available for your specific case. No offer is automatically applied — our staff will walk you through what is included.

How it works

Simple as 1, 2, 3

Vehicle financing is straightforward. Pay a portion upfront, a bank covers the rest, and you repay over time in fixed monthly installments.

01
Pay Your Down Payment
You contribute a minimum of 40% of the vehicle value upfront (50% for used vehicles). This is your down payment — the share you own from day one.
02
Bank Covers the Rest
A bank or financial institution lends you the remaining amount — up to 60% of the vehicle value (50% for used vehicles). They handle the disbursement directly.
03
Repay in Monthly EMI
You repay the loan in Equated Monthly Installments (EMI) — fixed amounts spread over your chosen tenure, from 5 to 10 years depending on the vehicle and lender.
Eligibility

Will I Qualify?

Most lenders look at a few basic things. If you have a steady income and can make the down payment, you are likely in a good position to apply.

Steady Income
Salaried, self-employed, or business income — lenders assess your repayment capacity.
Job Stability
Consistent employment history or an established business demonstrates reliability.
Credit History
A clean credit record from CIB helps secure better rates and higher loan amounts.
Valid Documentation
Citizenship certificate, income proof, and Bluebook (for used vehicles) are standard requirements.
